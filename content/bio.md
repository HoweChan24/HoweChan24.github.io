I am a PhD student at Zhejiang University, advised by **Prof. Xuehao Feng**. My research focuses on **green shipping**, **maritime safety**, and **maritime logistics**, with particular interests in shipping emissions reduction, maritime risk governance, and sustainable development in port and shipping systems.

Previously, I received my master's degree in Management Science and Engineering from Shenzhen University, where I was advised by **Prof. Jihong Chen**; and my bachelor's degree in Engineering Management from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

My publications have appeared in journals including *Transport Policy*, *Marine Pollution Bulletin*, *Ocean & Coastal Management*, and *Cities*. According to Google Scholar, my work has an **H-index of 6** with **151 citations**.
